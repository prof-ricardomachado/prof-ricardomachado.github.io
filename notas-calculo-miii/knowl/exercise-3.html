Exercício 3.

Determine o domínio das seguintes funções vetoriais:
  1. \(\displaystyle f(t)=(t^2,\sqrt{t-1},\sqrt{5-t}).\)
  2. \(\displaystyle r(t) = (\sqrt{4-t^2}, e^{-3t}, \ln{t+1}).\)
  3. \(\displaystyle g(t)=\dfrac{t-2}{t+2}\vec{i}+\sin(t)\vec{j}+ln(9-t^2)\vec{k}.\)
  4. \(\displaystyle s(t)= \dfrac{\sqrt{t+2}}{t+2}\vec{i}+ 2^t \vec{j}+ \ln(9-t^2)\vec{k}.\)
  5. \(\displaystyle h(t)= (\ln(t), t, \sqrt{1-t^2}).\)
  6. \(\displaystyle F(t)= \left(\ln(5 -t^2), e^{-t}, \sqrt{\frac{t-2}{t+1}}\right).\)
  7. \(\displaystyle R(t)= \sqrt{t-4}\vec{i}+ \sqrt{4-t} \vec{j}.\)
