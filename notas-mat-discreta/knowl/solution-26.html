Solução 2.2.7.1.

Temos

\begin{align*} x_2 = \amp ~ x_1 + 1\\ x_3 = \amp ~ x_2 + 2\\ x_4 = \amp ~ x_3 + 3\\ \vdots~~ = \amp ~~~~ \vdots\\ x_n = \amp ~ x_{n-1} + (n-1). \end{align*}

Somando, resulta

\begin{align*} x_n = \amp~ x_1+1+2+3+\cdots+(n-1)\\ = \amp~ 1+2+3+\cdots+(n-1)\\ = \amp~ \frac{n(n-1)}{2}. \end{align*}
