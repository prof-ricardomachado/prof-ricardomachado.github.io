Sejam \(a,b,c,d\in\mathbb{R}\) não nulos, então:

  1. \(\dfrac{a}{b}+\dfrac{c}{b}=\dfrac{a+c}{b}\text{;}\)
  2. \(\dfrac{a}{b}+\dfrac{c}{d}=\dfrac{ad+bc}{bd}\text{;}\)
  3. \(\dfrac{a}{b}\cdot\dfrac{c}{d}=\dfrac{ac}{bd}\text{;}\)
  4. \(\dfrac{a\cdot d}{b\cdot d}=\dfrac{a}{b}\text{;}\)
  5. \(\left(\dfrac{a}{b}\right)\bigg/\left(\dfrac{c}{d}\right)=\dfrac{a}{b}\cdot\dfrac{d}{c}\text{.}\)
