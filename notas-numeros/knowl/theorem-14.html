Teorema 7.2.5.

Se \(z=|z|(\cos \theta +i\mbox{sen}\theta)\) e \(w=|w|(\cos\varphi+i\mbox{sen}\varphi)\text{,}\) então

  1. \(\displaystyle z\cdot w= |z||w|(\cos(\theta+\varphi)+i\sin(\theta+\varphi))\)
  2. Se \(w\neq 0\text{,}\) então \(\dfrac{z}{w}=\dfrac{|z|}{|w|}(\cos(\theta-\varphi)+i\sin(\theta-\varphi)).\)
