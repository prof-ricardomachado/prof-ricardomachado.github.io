Exercício 2.

Verifique se os seguintes conjuntos são L.I. ou L.D.

  1. \(S=\{(1,\,1,\,0),\,(1,\,0,\,1),\,(0,\,1,\,1)\}\subset \mathbb{R}^3\text{.}\)
  2. \(S=\{1+2x,\, x+ x^3,\, x^2+ x^3 \}\subset \mathcal{P}_3[x] (\mathbb{R})\text{.}\)
  3. \(S=\left\{ \left[\begin{array}{rr} 1 \amp 0 \\ 1 \amp 1 \end{array}\right],\, \left[\begin{array}{rr} -1 \amp 2 \\ 0 \amp 1 \end{array}\right],\, \left[\begin{array}{rr} 0 \amp -1 \\ 2 \amp 1 \end{array}\right],\, \left[\begin{array}{rr} 1 \amp 8 \\ 0 \amp 5 \end{array}\right]\right\}\text{.}\)
  4. \(S= \{(-1,\,1,\,0),\,(0,\,1,\,-2),\,(-2,\,3,\,1)\}\subset \mathbb{R}^3\text{.}\)
  5. \(S=\{(1,\,2,\,-1),\,(-1,\,1,\,0),\,(-3,\,0,\,1),\,(-2,\,-1,\,1) \}\subset \mathbb{R}^3\text{.}\)
  6. \(S=\{2x+2,\, -x^2 + x+3,\,x^2 + 2x\}\subset \mathcal{P}_2[x](\mathbb{R})\text{.}\)
