Exemplo 2.3.5.
Sejam \(A, B\in\mathcal M_{3\times 4}\) dadas por
\begin{equation*} A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \amp 4 \amp 3 \amp 1\\ 2 \amp 5 \amp 4 \amp 4\\ 1 \amp -3 \amp -2 \amp 5 \end{bmatrix} \quad\text{e}\quad B = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \amp 0 \amp 0 \amp 3\\ 0 \amp 1 \amp 0 \amp -2\\ 0 \amp 0 \amp 1 \amp 2 \end{bmatrix}. \end{equation*}
Mostre que \(B\sim A\text{.}\)
