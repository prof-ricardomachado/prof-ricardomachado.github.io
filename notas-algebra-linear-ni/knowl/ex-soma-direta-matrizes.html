Exemplo 4.3.4.

Considere o espaço vetorial real \(V\) das matrizes \(2\times 2\text{.}\) Seja

\begin{align*} U \amp= \left\{ \left[ \begin{array}{cc} a \amp b \\ 0 \amp 0 \end{array} \right] \in \mathcal M_{2\times 2} : a,b \in \mathbb R \right\}\\ W \amp= \left\{ \left[ \begin{array}{cc} 0 \amp 0 \\ c \amp d \end{array} \right] \in \mathcal M_{2\times 2} : c,d \in \mathbb R \right\} \end{align*}

Mostre que \(V = U \oplus W\text{.}\)
