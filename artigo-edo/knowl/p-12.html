Retrato de fase do sistema de EDOs, não linear:

\begin{equation} \begin{cases} \displaystyle\dfrac{dx}{dt} \amp \displaystyle= xa - \alpha xy = x(a-\alpha y)\\ \dfrac{dy}{dt} \amp \displaystyle = -cy + \gamma xy = y(-c+\gamma x) \end{cases}\tag{2.1} \end{equation}

ou do sistema linear:

\begin{equation} \frac{d}{dt} \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array}\right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 \amp \frac{-\alpha c }{\gamma} \\ \frac{\gamma a}{\alpha} \amp 0 \end{array}\right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x-\frac{c}{\gamma} \\ y-\frac{a}{\alpha} \end{array}\right).\tag{2.2} \end{equation}
