Solução 1.1.13.1.

\begin{align*} \sum_{k=1}^{5} {f(x^{\ast}_k) \, \Delta x} \amp = 1/5 \cdot \left[ f(1/10) + f(3/10) + f(1/2) + f(7/10) + f(9/10) \right]\\ \amp = 1/5 \cdot \left[ \sqrt{1/10} + \sqrt{3/10} + \sqrt{1/2} + \sqrt{7/10} + \sqrt{9/10} \right] \\ \amp = 0.6712 \text{.} \end{align*}
