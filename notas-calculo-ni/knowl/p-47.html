Para quaisquer \(x,y\in\mathbb{R}\text{,}\)
  1. \(|x|\geq 0\text{;}\)
  2. \(|x|=|-x|\text{;}\)
  3. \(-|x|\leq x\leq |x|\text{;}\)
  4. \(|x|^2=x^2\text{;}\)
  5. \(|x\cdot y|=|x|\cdot|y|\text{;}\)
  6. \(\left|\dfrac{x}{y}\right|=\dfrac{|x|}{|y|}, \text{ se } y\neq 0\text{;}\)
  7. Desigualdade triangular: \(|x+y|\leq |x|+|y|\text{.}\)
