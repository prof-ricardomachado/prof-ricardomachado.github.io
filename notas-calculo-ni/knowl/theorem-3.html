Teorema 1.7.1.

São válidas as seguintes identidades:

  1. \(\displaystyle cos(a-b)=cos(a)cos(b)+sen(a)sen(b)\)
  2. \(\displaystyle cos(a+b)=cos(a)cos(b)-sen(a)sen(b)\)
  3. \(\displaystyle sen(a-b)=sen(a)cos(b)-sen(b)cos(a)\)
  4. \(\displaystyle sen(a+b)=sen(a)cos(b)+sen(b)cos(a)\)
  5. \(\displaystyle sen(2a)=2sen(a)cos(b)\)
  6. \(\displaystyle cos(2a)=cos^2(a)-sen^2(a)\)
