Exercício 1.

Para cada função \(h\) determine \(f\) e \(g\) tais que \(h(x)=f(g(x))\text{.}\) Depois use a regra da cadeia para calcular a derivada de \(h\text{.}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle h(x) = sen(4x)\)
  2. \(\displaystyle h(x) = \sqrt{4+3x}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle h(x) = (1-x^{2})^{100}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle h(x) = tg(sen(x))\)
  5. \(\displaystyle h(x) = sen(e^{x})\)
  6. \(\displaystyle h(x) = e^{\sqrt{x}}\)
  7. \(\displaystyle h(x) = (x^{3}+4x)^{7}\)
  8. \(\displaystyle h(x) = (x^{2}-x+1)^{3}\)
  9. \(\displaystyle h(x) = \sqrt[3]{1+tg(x)}\)
  10. \(\displaystyle h(x) = \sqrt[4]{1+2x+x^{3}}\)
