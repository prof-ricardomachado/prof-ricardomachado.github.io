Teorema 4.1.7.

Sejam \(A\) e \(B\) eventos, então:
  1. \(P(A^c) = 1 - P(A)\text{;}\)
  2. \(P(\varnothing)=0\text{;}\)
  3. \(P(A\setminus B) = P(A) - P(A\cap B)\text{;}\)
  4. \(P(A\cup B) = P(A)+P(B) - P(A\cap B)\text{;}\)
  5. Se \(A\supset B\) então \(P(A) \geq P(B)\text{.}\)
