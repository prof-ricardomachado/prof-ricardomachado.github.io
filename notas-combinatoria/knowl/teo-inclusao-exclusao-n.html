Teorema 2.1.6.

Sejam \(A_1,A_2,\cdots,A_n\) conjuntos finitos. A cardinalidade de
\begin{equation} \bigcup_{i=1}^{n}A_i = A_1 \cup A_2 \cup \cdots \cup A_n \tag{2.1.1} \end{equation}
é dada por:
\begin{equation} \begin{cases} \displaystyle\#\bigcup_{i=1}^{n}A_i \amp \displaystyle= \sum_{i=1}^{n}\#A_i-\sum_{0 \lt i \lt j \leq n}\#(A_i\cap A_j)+\cdots\\ \amp ~~~~~ \displaystyle\cdots +(-1)^{n-1}\#(A_1\cap A_2\cap \cdots\cap A_n) \end{cases}\tag{2.1.2} \end{equation}
