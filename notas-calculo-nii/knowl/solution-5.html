Solução 1.2.6.1.

Fazendo \(u = 3x-5\text{,}\) temos \(du = 3dx\) ou \(dx = 1/3 du\text{.}\) Portanto,
\begin{equation*} \int \frac{dx}{(3x-5)^8} = \int \frac{1/3 du}{u^8} = \frac{1}{3} \int u^{-8} du = \frac{1}{3} \frac{u^{-7}}{-7} + c = \frac{-1}{21(3x-5)^7} + c\text{.} \end{equation*}
