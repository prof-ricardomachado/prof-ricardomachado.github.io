Parágrafo
Sob as devidas condições de diferenciabilidade, as derivadas parciais da função composta \(h(x,y)\) são:
\begin{equation*} \frac{\partial h}{\partial x} = \frac{\partial f}{\partial u} \cdot \frac{\partial u}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial f}{\partial v} \cdot \frac{\partial v}{\partial x} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \frac{\partial h}{\partial y} = \frac{\partial f}{\partial u} \cdot \frac{\partial u}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial f}{\partial v} \cdot \frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\text{.} \end{equation*}
