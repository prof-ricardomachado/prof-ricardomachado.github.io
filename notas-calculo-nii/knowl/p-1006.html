Parágrafo

A partir da derivada \(\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\text{,}\) obtemos:
\begin{equation*} \frac{\partial}{\partial x}\left(\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\right) = \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x \partial y} \quad \text{e} \quad \frac{\partial}{\partial y}\left(\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\right) = \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y^2}\text{.} \end{equation*}
