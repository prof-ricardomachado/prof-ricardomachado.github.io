Exercício 2.

Nos itens a seguir, calcular as integrais indefinidas.
  1. \(\displaystyle \int \frac{x^2}{x^2 + 1} dx\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \int \frac{x^2 + 1}{x^2} dx\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \int \frac{sen\,x}{cos^2x} dx\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \int \sqrt{\frac{9}{1 - x^2}} dx\)
  5. \(\displaystyle \int \sqrt{\frac{4}{x^4 - x^2}} dx\)
  6. \(\displaystyle \int \frac{8x^4 - 9x^3 + 6x^2 - 2x + 1}{x^2} dx\)
  7. \(\displaystyle \int (\frac{e^t}{2} + \sqrt{t} + \frac{1}{t}) dt\)
  8. \(\displaystyle \int cos\,\theta \cdot tg\,\theta \, d\theta\)
  9. \(\displaystyle \int (e^x - e^{-x}) dx\)
  10. \(\displaystyle \int (t + \sqrt{t} + \sqrt[3]{t} + \sqrt[4]{t} + \sqrt[5]{t}) dt\)
  11. \(\displaystyle \int \frac{x^{-1/3} - 5}{x} dx\)
  12. \(\displaystyle \int sec^2x(cos^3x + 1) dx\)
  13. \(\int \frac{dx}{(ax)^2 + a^2}\text{,}\) onde \(a \ne 0\) é constante.
  14. \(\displaystyle \int \frac{x^2 - 1}{x^2 + 1} dx\)
  15. \(\displaystyle \int \sqrt[3]{8(t - 2)^6(t + \frac{1}{2})^3} dt\)
  16. \(\displaystyle \int (e^t - \sqrt[4]{16t} + \frac{3}{t^3}) dt\)
  17. \(\displaystyle \int \frac{ln\,x}{x\,ln\,x^2} dx\)
  18. \(\displaystyle \int tg^2x \cdot cosec^2x \, dx\)
  19. \(\displaystyle \int (x - 1)^2 (x + 1)^2 dx\)
  20. \(\int \frac{dt}{(n - 1/2)t^n}\text{,}\) onde \(n \in \mathbb{Z}\text{.}\)
