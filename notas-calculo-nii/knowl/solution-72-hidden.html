Mantendo \(y\) constante e derivando em relação a \(x\text{:}\)
\begin{equation*} \frac{\partial f}{\partial x} = 4xy + 3y^2 - 4\text{.} \end{equation*}

\begin{equation*} \frac{\partial f}{\partial y} = 2x^2 + 6xy\text{.} \end{equation*}
