(v) \(\int_0^{\pi/2} \operatorname{sen} 2x \, dx\text{.}\) Fazendo a primitiva por substituição, obtemos \(G(x) = -\frac{1}{2}\cos 2x\text{.}\)
\begin{equation*} \int_0^{\pi/2} \operatorname{sen} 2x \, dx = \left. -\frac{1}{2}\cos 2x \right|_0^{\pi/2} = -\frac{1}{2}\cos \pi - (-\frac{1}{2}\cos 0) = \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{2} = 1\text{.} \end{equation*}
